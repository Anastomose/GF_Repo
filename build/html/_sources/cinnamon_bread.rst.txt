--------------------
Cinnamon Bread
--------------------

A morning treat.

Dry Ingredients
----------------

::


    1 1/2 C rice flour

    1/3 C potato starch

    1/4 C tapioca starch

    1/4 C nonfat milk powder

    1/3 C granulated sugar

    1 T xanthan gum

    1 T Yeast

    1 1/4 t salt

    2 t cinnamon

    1 1/4 cups raisins 300 ml


Wet Ingredients
----------------

::

    2 eggs

    1 egg white

    1 cup water

    2 tbsp vegetable oil

    1 tsp cider vinegar


Oven
-------
350°F for 35 to 45 minutes


Instructions
------------

Combine wets in a batter bowl, whisk together. Combine wets in mixer.

Combine wet and dry ingredients, once they're fully combined beat the dough for 4 to 5 minutes. Stop, hand stir raisins. 

Place into bread pan and let rise uncovered to at least the edge of the pan. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 350°F.

Bake for 35 to 45 minutes at 350 F. Remove from the pan and cool on a rack to prevent a soggy bottom.

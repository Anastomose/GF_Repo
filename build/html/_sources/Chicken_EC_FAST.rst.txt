
-------------------------------------------------
Chicken Enchilada Fastarole
-------------------------------------------------

Ingredients
-----------

::

    3 C cooked, shredded chicken

    16 oz can enchilada sauce

    2 C shredded Mexican cheese

    6-8 corn tortillas

    1 can refried beans

Oven
-----
375 F for 30 minutes or until cheese is browned

Instructions
-------------

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.

Pour enough enchilada sauce in the bottom of a 9 x 13 pyrex, then layer on corn tortillas.

Add the following:

    * Beans
    * Chicken
    * Cheese
    * Sauce 
    * Tortillas
    * Cheese
    * Sauce
    * END!!

Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for about 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 5 minutes, then broil for 2 minutes.

Allow the whole thing to rest before cutting and serving. Top with standard enchilada garnishes as needed (lime, sour cream, cilantro, and/or avocado).

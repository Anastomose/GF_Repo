---------------------
Layered Hummus Dip
---------------------

::

    8 oz hummus

    1/4 C chopped red and yellow cherry tomatoes

    1/4 C chopped cucumbers

    2 T crumbled feta

    1 Box rice thins

Instructions
-----------------

1. Spread hummus on the bottom of a shallow dish. Top with layers of tomatoes, cucumbers, and feta. Drizzle with olive oil.

2. Serve with rice thins"

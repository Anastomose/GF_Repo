-------------------------
Crockpot Pumpkin Pudding
-------------------------

Ingredients
-----------

::

    15 oz solid pumpkin

    12 oz evaporated milk

    3/4 C sugar

    1/2 C biscuit mix

    2 eggs, beaten

    2 T butter, melted

    2 1/2 t pumpkin pie spice

    2 t vanilla


Preparation
------------

Mix all ingredients in a well-greased crock pot.  

Cook 3 to 6 hours on low, or until 160 degrees.
